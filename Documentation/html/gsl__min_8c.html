Metaboflux 2.0
Metaboflux is a generic approach for predicting flux distribution in metabolic networks under multiple and various constraints deducted from the experiments, to increase the biological relevance of the model. Metaboflux aims to provide a complete environnement to study the interaction and constraints of a metabolic network.
